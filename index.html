About Me

Updated 12/17/2015

I was recently an undergrad at UT Austin. I majored in Pure Mathematics and Astronautical Engineering. I also work as a research assistant at Center for Space Research under the supervision of Dr. Bettadpur.

My main areas of interest lies inside the boundaries of geometry, topology, and algebra. I also get easily excited by cool applications of algebraic and differential geometry and topology (e.g. for finite element analyses and geometry processing in general), stochastic estimation and control (sensor fusion, computer vision, Robotics, and UAVs), fluid dynamics, rocks related stuff, as well as the broad discipline of scientific computing and machine learning.
